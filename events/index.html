SO YOU THINK YOU'RE WISE

The BIG Quiz was a 2-day quiz.This quiz had questions from all topics ranging from Technology and Mental Ability to Sports and Political News and much more. The quiz was played in teams of 2. The teams had to pass 2 preliminary rounds in order to reach the final round. The final round was where the real fun began. There were questions on famous brands and products, famous places, (in)famous events and personalities, and much more. To top it off, there was a rapid fire face-off between the top 2 teams, which decided the winner.

YOUR IMAGINATION IS YOUR CODE

Whether you have experience in programming or are looking to get started for the first time, the upcoming 2-day python workshop will put you on the fast track to honing your skills.In this, you’ll learn about how to get started (Session 1), how to run python programs, types and operations in python (Session 2),functions and modules with an introduction of OO programming(Session 3) and the projects will also be discussed(Session 4).Further the advantages and disadvantages that Python provides as a programming language, the essentials of programming in Python, and what tools are available to build applications in Python can also be learnt.The certificates will be provided by Sparklab Engineering System in association with TECHNICHE-2014, IIT GUWAHATI. A mega competition will be held a day after the workshop and the merit holders will be appreciated. The winners will get a chance to represent JUIT at the final round of TECHNOSIA-2014 in the month of september at IIT GUWAHATI and get a chance to win prizes worth 1 Lac Rupees.

TRACKING IS NOW FUN

A GPS (Global Positioning System) based Satellite Robotics workshop. It was a 2 days GPS based Robotic vehicle workshop where students learned the art of making robotic vehicle, Programming microcontrollers using embedded C, Feeding artificial intelligence in robotic vehicle, Communication protocols, Satellites and GPS, interfacing GPS module and many more.
